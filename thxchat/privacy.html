ThxChat Privacy Policy

Thank you for using ThxChat. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure online social environment. This privacy policy details how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information. Please read carefully to understand our privacy practices.

1. Information We Collect

ThxChat offers the following login methods:

  • Anonymous Guest Login: Users can use ThxChat without providing any personal information.
  • Registered Account Login: Users can choose to register an account by providing a username, password, and optional email address to keep personalized data or services from being lost.
  • Third-party Account Login: Users can log in using third-party platforms such as Google, Facebook, GitHub, etc., by providing necessary information provided by these platforms.

In addition, we may collect the following information:

  • IP address
  • Device information (such as device type, operating system)
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage logs (including access time, browsed pages, etc.)

2. How We Use Information

If you use registered account or third-party account login, ThxChat will use this information to provide personalized services to enhance your user experience. Additionally, we will use this information to:

  • Provide user support and troubleshooting
  • Conduct data analysis and research to improve our services
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends, evaluate and improve service quality

3. Data Security

We take various measures to protect the security of your data, including but not limited to:

  • Data encryption: All data in transit is encrypted.
  • Access control: Only authorized employees can access your data, and only to the minimum extent necessary to provide you with services.
  • Security audits: Regular security audits and vulnerability scans to ensure system security.
  • Emergency response: We have a comprehensive emergency response plan to promptly address potential data leaks.

4. Data Sharing

Except for user-selected third-party account login, ThxChat will not share your personal data with any third party. The third-party service providers we use must also comply with strict data protection regulations, such as Google Analytics and other tools.

5. User Rights

You have the following rights to your data:

  • Access: You can access your personal data at any time.
  • Modify: You can modify your personal information.
  • Delete: You can request deletion of your personal data.

To exercise these rights, please contact our customer support team.

6. Data Retention

ThxChat will retain your personal data indefinitely unless you request deletion. In some cases (such as legal requirements), we may retain your data for a longer period.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

ThxChat uses localStorage to store caching data such as login tokens to enhance user experience. We do not use cookies for user tracking.

8. Children's Privacy

ThxChat's services are not intended for children under 16. If we discover that we have collected personal information from children under 16, we will promptly delete such information.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes to the privacy policy will be posted on our website and notified to you via email. Please check this page regularly for updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or need more information, please contact our customer support team at support@thxchat.com.
